# Sales and Profit Analysis Project

# 📊 Overview

This project analyzes sales and profit data to uncover key business insights across different product brands. The goal was to identify top-performing brands in terms of revenue and evaluate their profitability.

# 🛠️ Tools Used

- Excel   

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

- Data cleaning and preparation using Excel  

- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to identify sales and profit trends  

- Comparison of revenue and profit across different brands  

- Identification of high-performing and underperforming products  

# 📈 Key Insights

- The brand with the highest sales did not generate the highest profit  

- Some high-revenue products had low profit margins  

- Certain brands performed better in profitability despite lower sales volume  

# 💡 Business Implications

- High sales do not always translate to high profit  

- Businesses should focus on profit margins, not just revenue  

- Strategic pricing or cost reduction may be needed for high-sales, low-profit products
